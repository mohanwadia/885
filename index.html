#UnlockThe885 🔒

The 885 is our local bus takes thousands of people a week where they need to go, yet it doesn't run on Sundays.

We're calling on the State Government to upgrade the
Route 885 (Glen Waverley to Springvale via Wanda St)
to a full 7-day service.

  • Vote for 7-day service
  • Join Our Facebook Group

We have the third most popular Bus Route that does not run on Sundays in Victoria

In fact, the 885 is the most popular bus route in the south-east that does not run on Sundays

  • Vote for 7-day service

There's a $15bn new train corridoor to the city

Yet the 885 bus route - the only way to access it from Wanda St - does't run on Sundays.

  • Vote for 7-day service

We're in a cost of living crisis.

But the cheapest method of transportation doesn't run when we need it to.

  • Learn More

The 885 deserves better service for so many reasons.

With your support, we'll make it happen.
